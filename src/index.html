LBW 2014, Talybont-on-Usk

The 2014 LBW is planned for 20th–26th July 2014 in the village of Talybont-on-Usk, Wales.

We have a hall

We have found a hall on the village on Talybont-on-Usk, Wales, the United Kingdom. The hall is Henderson Hall. It is well equipped. It is largish, has a kitchen, Internet connection, video projector and useful things like that. Interior pictures to come.

We have a village

Talybont-on-Usk, an agreeable village in Wales. It has a CAMRA award winning pub, a canal, a river, and the Brecon Beacons National Park almost on top of you.

Activities

There are both walks at altitude for active days, and walks along the river and canal for more relaxed days. There are also lots of less obvious things including cycling, canoeing and stargazing.

Travel

The local website has a handy travel guide.

By rail

The nearest railway station is at Abergavenny. Note that the town also has a Welsh name, Y Fenni. This should be a familiar concept to Belgians.

From Abergavenny, you can go …

By bus

The X43 bus runs through Talybont-on-Usk. It goes to Abergavenny (railway connection), Brecon, Merthyr Tydfil (or Merthyr Tudful, has railway connection), and Cardiff (or Caerdydd, has railway connection). The nearest National Express coach stop is at Abergavenny.

By canal

The Monmouthshire & Brecon Canal runs through the Talybont-on-Usk. Unfortunately the canal is not currently connected to the rest of the network. Boats can be hired. It runs near the town of Abergavenny, which can be reached by railway.

By car

Point your sat-nav at the postcode LD3 7YQ.

There is local car rental, include an electric car.

By air

Nearby airports are Cardiff, Sheffield and Manchester.

What is the LBW?

LBW is a week-long event which takes place in a different European country each summer, drawing together Open Source software enthusiasts from more than a dozen different countries, for a combination of talks, presentations, hands-on mini-projects, outdoor exercise, and good food and drink.
